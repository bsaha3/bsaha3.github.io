About

AI in health:
o Building tools/apps/softwares to power evidence based care by using clinical practices and patient data
o Building a large scale knowledge graph for PubMed
o Building a bioportal to connect researchers with bio entities and develop AI algorithms to enable better expert finding

AI in medicine:
o Building a large scale knowledge graph by integrating 20+ datasets in drug discovery
o Developing deep graph mining algorithms for drug discovery

Data-Driven Science of Science:
o Developing entitymetrics by applying bibliometric methods to medicine so as to facilitate scientific discovery
o Measuring the pace of AI innovations from the perspectives of innovation diffusion, team collaboration, labor division, and diversity
o Selfish knowledge: understanding scientific success and self-promotions

Ai Health Data Challenge

Big changes are on the horizon for all of healthcare. Access to ever-increasing amounts of data demands ever-evolving tools to harness and control this data for expert patient care. Where do these tools come from? Who can tame these unwieldy data sets? How can patient care be enhanced? AI Health wants to answer these questions through our Data Challenge. AI Health is proud to issue the call to the students of The University of Texas to rise to this challenge and join us in providing cutting edge technology in evidence-based care that will be part of the changing face of healthcare.
